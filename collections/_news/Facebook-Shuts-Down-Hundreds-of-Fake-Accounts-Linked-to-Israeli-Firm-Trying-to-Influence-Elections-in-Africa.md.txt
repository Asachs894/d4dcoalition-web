---

A private Israeli firm called Archimedes Group [has become][link] the latest member of a growing number of private entities that have been revealed to be employing disinformation on Facebook in an effort to sway elections. This time, the targeted elections were those that recently took place in countries in Sub-Saharan Africa, as well as a small number of Latin American and Southeast Asian states. Though Facebook [confirmed that it has removed pages and accounts][link 2] linked to the Archimedes Group, it has not stated what individual, group, or government, if any, had hired the company to disrupt the recent polls in places like Nigeria, Senegal, and Togo.
